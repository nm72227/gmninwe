The Touqas Legacy Blueprint

A Symphony of Passion, Taste, and Quality Living

Founded on the foundational creed of “good food, great living”, TOUQAS FOODS arose not merely as a restaurant space, but as a deliberate response to the modern urban demand for high-tier gastronomic perfection. We believe fast food shouldn't mean compromised quality or chemical additives. Every spice blend is calculated, every vegetable leaf trace-washed, and every burger smash designed for juicy complexity.

Our journey is deeply embedded in community validation, creating premium spaces where friends can unwind over gourmet artisan momos and crisp hand-broasted meals. We blend tech-driven fast ordering with classic slow-marinated kitchen integrity.
